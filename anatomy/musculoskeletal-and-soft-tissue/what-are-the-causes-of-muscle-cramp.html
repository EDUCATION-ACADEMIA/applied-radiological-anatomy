What are the causes of muscle cramp?

(A) ** Lack of warming up

(B) ** Accumulation of lactic acid

(C) ** Lack of potassium and sodium ion

(D) Hot weather

(E) Lack of energy

Concept note-1: -Muscle weakness and cramps They can occur when blood potassium levels are low and can be painful. Within skeletal muscle, potassium helps relay signals from your brain to stimulate contractions. It also helps end these contractions by leaving the muscle cells.
