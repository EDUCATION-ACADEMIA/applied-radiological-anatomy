How do the skeletal system and the lymphatic system work together

(A) ** the bone marrow white blood cells that the lymphatic system uses

(B) the bones provide places for the capillaries to rest

(C) the bones provide places for the capillaries to rest

(D) the two systems don’t work together.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -They include protecting your body from illness-causing invaders, maintaining body fluid levels, absorbing digestive tract fats and removing cellular waste.

Concept note-2: -The lymphatic system does different things: it acts as a one-way drainage system – this means it moves fluid from body tissues into the blood circulation. it contains white blood cells called lymphocytes, which fight infection. it gets rid of any waste that cells make.

Concept note-3: -managing the fluid levels in the body. reacting to bacteria. dealing with cancer cells. dealing with cell products that otherwise would result in disease or disorders. absorbing some of the fats in our diet from the intestine. 19-Jul-2017
