____ is the study of how body parts function.

(A) Biology

(B) Anatomy

(C) ** Physiology

(D) Pathophysiology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Anatomy refers to the internal and external structures of the body and their physical relationships, whereas physiology refers to the study of the functions of those structures.

Concept note-2: -Physiology is the study of how the various parts of the body work together. This includes the molecules, cells, organs, muscles and all of their chemical processes and functions.

Concept note-3: -Anatomy is the science that studies the structure of the body.

Concept note-4: -Body functions are the physiological or psychological functions of body systems. The body’s functions are ultimately its cells’ functions. Survival is the body’s most important business. Survival depends on the body’s maintaining or restoring homeostasis, a state of relative constancy, of its internal environment.
