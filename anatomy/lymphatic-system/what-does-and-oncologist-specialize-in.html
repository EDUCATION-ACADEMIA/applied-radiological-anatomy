What does and Oncologist specialize in?

(A) radiation therapy

(B) chemotherapy

(C) blood disorders

(D) ** cancer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A medical oncologist is a specialist who uses chemotherapy and other drugs, such as targeted treatment or immunotherapy, to treat cancer. He oversees the creation of the patient’s therapy as the patient’s primary health practitioner.

Concept note-2: -Who is an Oncologist? Oncology is the specialized branch of medicine devoted to the field of cancer including diagnosis, treatments, and researches. Doctors who treat cancer patients are known as oncologists.

Concept note-3: -A doctor who has special training in diagnosing and treating cancer in adults using chemotherapy, hormonal therapy, biological therapy, and targeted therapy. A medical oncologist often is the main health care provider for someone who has cancer.
