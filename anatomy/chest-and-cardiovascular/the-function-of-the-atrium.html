The function of the atrium

(A) ** To collect blood

(B) To pump blood to the lungs

(C) To pump blood into systemic circulation

(D) To pump blood to the heart muscle

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The function of the right atrium is to receive deoxygenated blood from the body. It delivers this to the right ventricle which pumps this blood to the lungs for oxygenation. The function of the left atrium is to receive oxygenated blood from the lungs.

Concept note-2: -The right atrium receives blood from the veins and pumps it to the right ventricle. The right ventricle pumps the blood received from the right atrium to the lungs. The left atrium receives oxygenated blood from the lungs and pumps it to the left ventricle.
