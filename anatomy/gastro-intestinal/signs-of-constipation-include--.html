Signs of constipation include ____ ?

(A) Liquid or frequent stools

(B) tachycardia or rapid heart rate

(C) Fecal incontinence

(D) ** Irritability

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the flip side, not pooping enough can affect our physical wellbeing and our mood too. Science has shown that people with constipation are more likely to be diagnosed with mood and anxiety problems.

Concept note-2: -Irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) is a common condition that affects the digestive system. It causes symptoms like stomach cramps, bloating, diarrhoea and constipation. These tend to come and go over time, and can last for days, weeks or months at a time. It’s usually a lifelong problem.

Concept note-3: -fewer than three bowel movements a week. stools that are hard, dry, or lumpy. stools that are difficult or painful to pass. a feeling that not all stool has passed.

Concept note-4: -Passing fewer than three stools a week. Having lumpy or hard stools. Straining to have bowel movements. Feeling as though there’s a blockage in your rectum that prevents bowel movements. Feeling as though you can’t completely empty the stool from your rectum. More items •31-Aug-2021
